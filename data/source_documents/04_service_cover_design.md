# Service: Genre-Aligned Cover Design

## Overview
Cinematic book cover design and comprehensive author branding. Our designers create stunning covers that grab readers' attention, reflect the emotional tone of the story, and comply with genre tradition.

## Key Benefits
- **Typography & Imagery**: Custom typography and high-resolution artwork optimized for both digital thumbnails and luxury print layouts.
- **Author Branding**: Creation of professional author logos, branding consistency, and assets for marketing.
- **Composition**: Layouts designed using established composition rules (like the rule of thirds) to maximize visual engagement.

## Service Details
- **Service Page Path**: `/services/cover-design`
- **Output Formats**: Digital ebook covers, print wrap covers (paperback/hardcover), and marketing graphic assets.
