# Service: Copyright & Rights Protection

## Overview
Intellectual property security and official rights registration. We handle the technical and legal paperwork required to officially register your book's copyright, ensuring you maintain legal evidence of ownership.

## Key Benefits
- **Official Registration**: Handling applications with the US Copyright Office (or local equivalents).
- **Rights Protection**: Legal frameworks to protect creative property from piracy and unauthorized adaptations.
- **Global Protection**: Secures international distribution protection under copyright treaties.

## Service Details
- **Service Page Path**: `/services/copyright-registration`
- **Ownership Note**: Authors maintain 100% legal ownership of their rights; this service simply secures that protection officially.
