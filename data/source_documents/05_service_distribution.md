# Service: Global Book Distribution Strategy

## Overview
Enterprise-grade distribution pipelines that sync your book to over 40,000 retail nodes across 180+ countries. We manage all technical setups for global distribution.

## Key Benefits
- **Retail Reach**: Your book is made available on Amazon, Apple Books, Barnes & Noble, Kobo, and local bookstores worldwide.
- **Key Channels**: Full integration with KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing) and IngramSpark setup.
- **Distribution Speed**: Global setup can be processed and completed in hours rather than weeks.

## Service Details
- **Service Page Path**: `/services/print-on-demand-setup`
- **Modality**: Print-on-demand (POD) and digital ebook global distribution.
