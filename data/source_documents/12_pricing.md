# Pricing & Payment Structure

## Modular Pricing Model
We believe in absolute transparency and flexibility. Therefore, we do not lock you into rigid, one-size-fits-all bundles. Our services are entirely **modular and a la carte**. 

You choose and pay only for the exact services you need for your manuscript. For example, if you already have a stunning cover, you can hire us solely for developmental editing and global distribution.

## Estimating Your Project Cost
Since word counts, design requirements, and marketing goals vary widely across books:
- Pricing is calculated based on the scope of your manuscript (e.g. word count, complexity).
- To get a exact quote, we offer a **free initial consultation** where our consultants evaluate your project and prepare a tailored project proposal.

## Royalty Payment Flow
- **100% Royalties to Author**: All royalties from sales on Amazon (KDP), Apple, Kobo, and IngramSpark are paid directly to the author's bank account.
- **Zero Commission**: We never take a percentage of your sales. Our fees are strictly project-based.
