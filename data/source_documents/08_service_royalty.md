# Service: Royalty Intelligence

## Overview
Integrated sales tracking and automated royalty accounting systems. We configure your dashboard pipelines so you can track sales across multiple international platforms in real-time, receiving transparent and direct payouts.

## Key Benefits
- **Direct Payouts**: 100% of royalties are paid directly from retail platforms to your account.
- **Unified Dashboard**: Real-time sales tracking and royalty reporting across Amazon, IngramSpark, and other retailers.
- **Financial Transparency**: Accurate royalty accounting reports for co-authors or tax purposes.

- **Service Page Path**: `/services/royalty-accounting`
- **Fee Model**: Zero commission. We charge a one-time setup fee, taking 0% of your book sales revenue.
