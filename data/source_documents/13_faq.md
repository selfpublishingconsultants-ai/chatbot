# Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who keeps the rights to my book?
A: You retain 100% of your creative and legal rights. We act purely as your service provider and consultant. We do not take any percentage of your rights or ownership.

Q: How long does the publishing process take?
A: The timeline varies based on your manuscript's condition and the services you select. Once formatted and approved, global distribution can be completed in hours rather than weeks.

Q: How do I get paid from sales?
A: Authors receive direct payouts from sales platforms with transparent, real-time reporting.


Q: Do you guarantee book sales?
A: We provide high-impact launch strategies and marketing campaigns to maximize reach and conversion, but we do not guarantee specific sales counts.

Q: Can I choose only the services I need?
A: Yes. All our services are modular. You select only what you need (e.g. just editing or just formatting).
