# 2026 Self-Publishing Standards & Industry Trends

## Compliance Requirements
1. **AI Disclosure**: US Copyright Office mandates disclosure for books using AI. We ensure full compliance.
2. **Audiobooks**: Amazon's Virtual Voice narrator model applies a 40% royalty structure.
3. **Transparency**: We guarantee 100% human-verified formatting to avoid automated platform flags.
4. **Fee Elimination**: On Feb 1st, 2026, Amazon eliminated the $25 print cover and interior revision fees.

## Industry Trends
- **Rights Ownership**: Strong trend of authors keeping 100% rights, avoiding hybrid publisher models.
- **Speed**: Global distribution is active in hours rather than weeks.
- **Discoverability**: High emphasis on category and semantic search optimization over keyword stuffing.
