# Service: Book Marketing & Launch Strategy

## Overview
High-impact book marketing campaigns and launch strategies customized to your book's target audience. We build a comprehensive promotion strategy to build momentum and reach readers.

1. **Targeted Advertising**: Configuring and running paid ad campaigns on Amazon Advertising and Meta (Facebook/Instagram).
2. **Launch Campaigns**: Orchestrating a launch schedule designed to gain early reviews and ranking momentum.
3. **Email Marketing**: Building reader lists, creating automated campaigns, and distributing promotional material.
4. **Social Media & Influencer Outreach**: Coordinating with genre-specific bloggers, reviewers, and influencers.
